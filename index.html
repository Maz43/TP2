Frequently Asked Questions

What are the check-in and check-out times?

Check-in is at 3:00 PM and check-out is at 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out can be requested based on availability.

Are pets allowed in the hotel?

Yes, we are a pet-friendly hotel. A pet fee applies and we offer specialized pet amenities.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 48 hours prior to arrival are fully refundable. Standard rates apply for late cancellations.

Do you offer airport transportation?

Yes, we offer luxury airport transfers. Please contact our concierge to arrange this prior to your arrival.
